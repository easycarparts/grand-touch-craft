# Tint, PPF, and Ceramic Business Findings

Date: 2026-07-08

This document captures the working assumptions, pricing math, funnel structure, and ad angles discussed for Grand Touch Auto's tint push. Numbers are working estimates and should be updated weekly from actual CRM, ad, and booking data.

## Situation Summary

PPF is currently under heavy price pressure. Competitors are anchoring full-body ForceShield-type PPF around AED 7,000, while Grand Touch's current public package is around AED 10,990. If competitors are genuinely offering similar film, prep, warranty, finish, and aftercare, then the issue is not only positioning. It is a cost-structure and market-anchor problem.

The immediate business goal is to create faster-turnaround services that keep bays active, soak up rent and staff costs, and reduce reliance on low-close, high-ticket PPF leads.

Priority order:

1. Window tint as the cash-flow product.
2. Tint add-ons and light detailing as margin boosters.
3. Ceramic coating as an upsell or later funnel, not the first new main funnel.
4. PPF only where margin is protected or the job feeds profitable bundles.

| Item | Cost / Value |
|---|---:|
| Revenue | AED 10,000 |
| Film/material | AED 4,000 |
| Installer contractor | AED 2,400 |

Conclusion: under the current contractor model and ad cost, PPF at AED 10,000 is close to break-even or loss-making. It is high revenue but weak contribution unless pricing, labor cost, CPA, or upsells improve.

PPF staff break-even:

| Scenario | Break-even Cars / Month |
|---|---:|
| AED 10,000/month total PPF staff cost replacing AED 2,400 contractor/tint cost per car | 5 cars/month |
| AED 20,000/month PPF staff cost replacing AED 2,400 contractor/tint cost per car | 9 cars/month |
| Loaded AED 24,000-26,000/month staff cost | 10-11 cars/month |

With 2 PPF staff doing roughly 1 car every 2 days, practical capacity is around 13 cars/month. The model only works if those staff are kept near 70-85% utilized.

## Tint Pricing Structure

Recommended public pricing:

| Vehicle Type | Essential Tint | STEK Smart Ceramic | Premium Heat Rejection |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Hatchback / Small | AED 599-649 | AED 1,199-1,299 | AED 1,899 |
| Sedan | AED 699-799 | AED 1,399-1,499 | AED 2,199-2,399 |
| Simple Coupe / Sports | AED 699-899 | AED 1,399-1,499 | AED 2,199-2,499 |
| SUV / 4x4 | AED 799-999 | AED 1,599-1,699 | AED 2,499-2,799 |
| Exotic / Complex Sports | Quote by model | AED 1,899+ | AED 3,000+ |

Pricing rule: price by glass area, difficulty, and liability. Do not blindly price every sports car above SUVs because many sports cars use less material. Charge more only for exotic/high-liability/complex installs.

Main seller: STEK Smart Ceramic.

Budget product: Essential / Action, used as a price-shopper catcher.

Premium upsell: STEK Nex or V-Kool-type premium only if margin remains strong.

## Ceramic Coating Position

Ceramic coating is not the first priority as a standalone funnel because current selling price is often only AED 1,000-1,500 and it can consume 1-2 days of bay time.

Recommended use:

| Role | Offer |
|---|---|
| Tint upsell | Quick exterior protection/detail from AED 499-999 |
| PPF upsell | Ceramic topper / aftercare bundle |
| Later standalone funnel | Express Gloss & Protection from AED 999-1,499 |

Do not lead with broad "ceramic coating" ads until the offer is tighter and the bay-time economics are proven.

## Funnel Strategy

The tint funnel should act like a product selector, not a generic "cheap tint" page.

Recommended flow:

1. Pick vehicle size.
2. Pick film tier.
3. See price.
4. Add optional windshield / roof film.
5. Enter name and WhatsApp.
6. Pick preferred timing: Today, Tomorrow, This week, Just checking.
7. Submit / WhatsApp handoff.

The live `/tint-dubai` funnel now captures:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Vehicle size | Price and qualification |
| Tint tier | Package selection |
| Add-ons | Average ticket uplift |
| Final price | CRM estimate and ad value |
| Preferred install timing | Sales prioritization |
| Name and WhatsApp | Lead capture |

CRM notes:

| Field | Stored As |
|---|---|
| Add-ons | `selected_extras` |
| Install timing | `preferred_install_timing`, `preferred_install_timing_label` |
| Price | `final_price`, `service_price`, `estimate_value` |

The admin lead expanded panel shows install timing so sales can prioritize Today / Tomorrow leads.

## Creative Strategy

Use video first for Meta because tint needs proof and education. Static image ads can support retargeting and price reminders.

Creative split:

| Creative Type | Role |
|---|---|
| Video | Cold traffic, education, heat problem, trust |
| Image | Retargeting, offer reminder, package comparison |

Do not overproduce the first batch. Phone-shot vertical videos are enough if the hook is clear, the offer is clear, and the CTA sends to the funnel.

Core repeated message:

> Genuine STEK ceramic tint. Installed in around 3 hours. From AED 1,199. Legal shades. Warranty registered. Price shown before WhatsApp.

Use price in most ads. Do not hide the price, because the funnel is built around price clarity. Mention price by clip 3 or 4 in cold ads, earlier in retargeting.

Use STEK and authorised/warranty as selling points only if authorisation applies to STEK window film/tint, not only PPF.

Avoid offering free pickup on tint unless the operation is willing to absorb that cost. Prefer:

> Installed at our DIP 2 studio in around 3 hours.

Important: avoid directly naming or accusing a specific dealer. Keep the claim framed as a common risk and use "often" / "can" language. The point is to educate and redirect, not look bitter.

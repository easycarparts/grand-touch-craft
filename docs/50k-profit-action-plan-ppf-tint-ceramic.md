# 50k AED Monthly Profit Action Plan: PPF, Tint, Ceramic

Created for the 30-60 day push to keep PPF healthy while using window tint and ceramic coating to reduce monthly pressure.

## Goal

The target is not to replace PPF. The target is to stop PPF from carrying the entire business alone.

Monthly goal:

| Metric | Target |
|---|---:|
| Revenue | 200k-250k AED |
| Owner profit | 40k-50k AED |
| PPF volume | 10-15 cars |
| Tint volume | 35-45 cars |
| Ceramic volume | 10-12 cars |
| Add-on revenue | 8k-15k AED |

The business needs roughly 82k AED contribution before fixed overhead to make 50k AED owner profit realistic.

## Current Reality

PPF CPL is strong at roughly 22-30 AED on the lead funnel. That means the funnel is not the main issue.

The current pressure comes from:

- PPF material prices increasing.
- ForceShield becoming harder to sell at 10k AED.
- Contractor install cost reducing margin.
- PPF jobs taking multiple days.
- One service line carrying rent, staff, ads, and owner income.

Tint and ceramic should create daily cashflow and margin so PPF can be sold with more discipline.

## PPF Plan

Keep the current PPF funnel live. A 22-30 AED CPL is good. Do not rebuild or panic-change PPF ads unless lead quality collapses.

Use this price ladder:

| Offer | Target Price |
|---|---:|
| Track pack / high-impact areas | 2,999-3,999 AED |
| Full front | 4,500-5,500 AED |
| Full body ForceShield gloss | 8,499-9,499 AED |
| Full body ForceShield matte | 9,499-10,500 AED |
| STEK / Dyno premium | 11,500+ AED |

Rules:

- Do not sell bare full PPF below margin floor just to fill the bay.
- If a client pushes price down, attach Smart tint or ceramic top coat.
- Treat STEK/Dyno as premium brand-buyer products only.
- Review PPF lead quality weekly by qualified rate, quote rate, booked rate, and margin.

Sales line:

> Full PPF starts from AED 8,499, but most clients package it with Smart tint or ceramic top coat while the car is already in.

## Tint Plan

Tint is the daily cashflow product.

Offer ladder:

| Product | Small | Medium | SUV / Large | Role |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| STEK Action | 649-699 | 799 | 999 | Entry price only |
| STEK Smart Ceramic | 1,299 | 1,499 | 1,699 | Main seller |
| STEK NEX | 2,199 | 2,399 | 2,799 | Premium upsell |
| Clear windshield heat film | +499 | +499 | +499 | Add-on |
| Panoramic roof tint | +699 | +699 | +699 | Add-on |

Rules:

- Smart is the default recommendation.
- Action is the decoy or budget save.
- NEX is for luxury, family cars, heat-sensitive drivers, and premium clients.
- Offer windshield film on every quote.
- Take 100-200 AED deposit for bookings once volume rises.

First 30-day target:

- 25-35 tint bookings if campaign is still learning.
- 35-45 tint bookings if CPL and close rate hold.
- Average ticket above 1,500 AED.
- Booked CPA under 350 AED if possible; under 600 AED still workable.

## Ceramic Plan

Ceramic is the margin filler and upsell product.

Offer ladder:

| Offer | Price |
|---|---:|
| Basic exterior ceramic | From 1,500 AED |
| Proper paint correction plus ceramic | From 2,500 AED |
| Full bundle: exterior, interior, rims, glass | 2,999-3,499 AED |

Rules:

- Do not lead with cheap coating unless bays are empty.
- Use ceramic first as retargeting and upsell.
- Offer ceramic on every tint booking.
- Offer ceramic top coat, wheels, or interior ceramic on every PPF booking.

Campaign:

- Start with retargeting at 50-100 AED/day.
- Audience: tint visitors, PPF visitors, IG engagers, WhatsApp clickers.
- Offer: paint correction plus ceramic from 2,500 AED.
- Use real workshop before/after and gloss videos.

Sales line:

> Since the car is already in, we can add ceramic from AED 1,500, or proper correction plus coating from AED 2,500.

## Sales Process

Response time target:

- Reply within 5 minutes during working hours.
- No lead older than 24 hours without a real touch.

Tint script:

1. Confirm car model.
2. Ask if they want comfort/heat rejection or just darkness.
3. Recommend Smart Ceramic as default.
4. Offer windshield film.
5. Offer ceramic if the car is already coming in.
6. Ask for 100-200 AED deposit to hold slot.
7. Confirm arrival time, pickup/drop, and expected completion.

Follow-up cadence:

| Touch | Timing | Message |
|---|---|---|
| First | Within 5 min | Confirm car and recommend package |
| Second | 2-3 hours later | Offer specific slot |
| Third | Next morning | Hold one final slot and ask yes/no |

CRM discipline:

- New
- Contacted
- Quoted
- Booked
- Won
- Lost
- Junk

If statuses are not updated, CPL and CPA become unreliable.

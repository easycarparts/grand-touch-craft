# UAE Business Citations — Freelancer Brief

**Goal:** Create consistent, high-quality local citations for our business across authoritative UAE directories. This is a **local SEO citation** job, NOT a "mass backlink" job. Consistency and quality matter more than volume.

---

## 1. NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES (read first)

1. **Every listing must use the EXACT business details below** — same spelling, same spacing, same phone format. Do not abbreviate, reorder, or "improve" anything.
2. **No spun/reworded descriptions.** Use the provided short or long description verbatim.
3. **Only submit to the sites on the approved list (Section 3).** Do NOT add extra directories to inflate the count.
4. **Skip any site that is dead, parked, spam, or requires payment.** Note it as "skipped" — don't force it.
5. **Deliverable = a tracking sheet** (Section 4) with the LIVE listing URL for every submission. "Submitted" without a URL doesn't count.

## 5. HOW TO HIRE THE RIGHT PERSON (notes for us, not the freelancer)

- **Search Fiverr for:** "UAE business listing", "UAE local citations", "GMB + citations".
- **Budget:** ~$15–40 for a curated ~25-site job. Do NOT buy "500 directories for $5" gigs.
- **Before ordering, message the seller:** "Will you use my exact business details on every listing and return a sheet with live URLs and login credentials? I have a fixed list of ~25 approved directories." A good seller says yes.
- **Red flags:** promises of "high DA backlinks", "instant ranking", auto-generated reports without real URLs, refusal to share logins.
- **After delivery:** spot-check 5 random live URLs to confirm the NAP matches exactly.
